Share a Davis station to the Corteva Weather Pillar

  1. Log in at https://www.weatherlink.com/
  2. Click the Station icon
  3. Click Share & Uploads
  4. In the pop-up, select Add Share
  5. Search Corteva Weather and click Search
  6. Leave default Basic Share, then click Share
