Breaking News October 2025

Corteva to Separate Into Two Companies

Corteva Agriscience announced plans to split into two independent, publicly traded companies. The seed business, to be named "SpinCo," will be home to Pioneer brand and is expected to generate $9.9 billion in net sales. Current CEO Chuck Magro will lead SpinCo. The separation is expected in the second half of 2026.

Yield Contest December 2024

David Hula Wins 2024 NCGA Contest with Pioneer

Virginia farmer David Hula topped the 2024 National Corn Yield Contest with 490.63 bu/acre using Pioneer P14830VYHR. Pioneer products were used in 310 entries exceeding 300 bu/acre - the most of any seed brand.
