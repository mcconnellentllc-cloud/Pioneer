Corn Impact Notes — Haxtun

July Heat Stress: 9 days at or above 95°F during tasseling window. Pollen viability drops significantly above 95°F. Night temps above 70°F also reduce kernel set.
GDU Pace: Season accumulation of 2,685 GDUs. Full-season hybrids (108-day) typically need 2,700-2,800 GDUs. Short-season hybrids (100-day) need ~2,400 GDUs.
Water Deficit: Season ET of 28.4" with only 11.2" precipitation = 17.2" deficit. Irrigated acres needed approximately 14-16" of applied water to meet demand.
Frost Window: Last spring frost May 4, first fall frost Sep 28 = 147 frost-free days. Adequate for 100-108 day hybrids planted by mid-May.
