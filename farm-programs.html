Action Required Deadline: February 28, 2026

Verify Your Login.gov Account Before FSA Rollout

FSA is transitioning to Login.gov for all online services. Producers must verify their accounts before February 28 to maintain access to FSA programs and services.

Complete These Steps Now

  1. Create a Login.gov account — Go to login.gov and create an account using your email address
  2. Create a Farmers.gov account — Visit farmers.gov and register using your Login.gov credentials
  3. Link your accounts — Connect your Login.gov account to your existing FSA customer records
  4. Verify your identity — Complete identity verification at your local FSA office or participating Post Office

Questions? Contact your local FSA office or call Kyle at (970) 571-1015 for assistance.
