How to Enroll

  1. Contact Kyle at 970-571-1015 or kyle.mcconnell@plantpioneer.com to request access
  2. Provide your field information — Kyle will need your field locations and pivot details
  3. Receive your login credentials — You'll get access to the Water Reporter portal
  4. Complete a brief walkthrough — Kyle will show you how to read reports and use the tool

How to Set Up Your Fields

  1. Your field boundaries are pulled from Granular or Field360 — make sure your boundaries are accurate
  2. Identify your irrigation zones — which pivots cover which fields
  3. Provide crop information for each field (corn, soybeans, etc.)
  4. Share soil type data if available — helps calculate water holding capacity
  5. Connect pivot telemetry if you have it — enables automatic irrigation tracking

How to Use Water Reporter

  1. Log in at wreporter.pd.research.corteva.com using your grower credentials
  2. Select your operation and field from the dashboard
  3. Review the soil moisture map — colors show water availability across the field
  4. Check the irrigation recommendation — when to water and how much
  5. Compare forecasted weather to see if rain is expected
  6. Track your as-applied irrigation against recommendations
  7. Monitor water use efficiency throughout the season
