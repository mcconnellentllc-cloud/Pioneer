Getting Started with ChatGPT

Your go-to AI for creating images, videos, and social media content. Build a professional online presence without hiring a marketing agency.

Need a logo? A Facebook post? A video idea? Don't hire anyone — ask ChatGPT. AI creates professional marketing content in minutes. Better and faster than any agency.

What Is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is an AI assistant made by OpenAI. It's probably the most well-known AI tool out there. What makes ChatGPT stand out is its ability to create images and help with visual content. It can:

  • Generate images — professional photos, logos, graphics from a text description
  • Write social media posts — Facebook, Instagram, X posts with the right tone and length
  • Create marketing content — flyers, ad copy, email campaigns
  • Plan a content calendar — what to post, when to post it, and why
  • Help with video ideas — scripts, shot lists, and concepts for short videos

The free plan lets you do a lot. The paid plan ($20/month) gives you more image generation and the latest features.

How to Get Started

1

Go to ChatGPT

Open your web browser and go to:

chatgpt.com

There's also a phone app for iPhone and Android — search "ChatGPT" in your app store.

2

Create a Free Account

Click "Sign Up" and use your email, Google account, or Apple account. The free plan is enough to get started and try everything out.

3

Start Creating

Type what you want in the message box. For images, just describe what you want to see. For text content, describe what you need. ChatGPT understands plain English — no special commands needed.

Example: Build a Social Media Presence for Your Business

Let's walk through creating a complete social media setup for a small ag business. We'll create images, write posts, and plan a content schedule — all from scratch.

1

Create a Professional Cover Photo

Every Facebook business page needs a good cover photo. Let ChatGPT make one for you.

You Type: Create a professional Facebook cover photo for a farm services business called "McConnell Enterprises" in Haxtun, Colorado. Show a beautiful wheat field at golden hour with rolling plains in the background. Make it look professional and clean — no text on the image. Wide landscape format.
ChatGPT Responds With: A high-quality AI-generated image of a golden wheat field at sunset with Colorado plains. You can download it directly and upload it to your Facebook page. If you don't love it, just say "try again with more green" or "make it more dramatic" — ChatGPT will generate a new version.
Tip: AI-generated images are great for backgrounds, cover photos, and social media graphics. For photos of your actual equipment or farm, use your phone — those real photos build trust with customers.
2

Write a Week of Facebook Posts

Ask ChatGPT to batch-create a whole week of content.

You Type: I run McConnell Enterprises, a Pioneer seed dealership and farm services business in Haxtun, Colorado. Write me 5 Facebook posts for this week. Keep them short, friendly, and professional. Include: one about spring planting prep, one about our Pioneer seed lineup, one asking customers to share their planting pics, one about a farming tip, and one weekend post that's more personal and community-focused. Add relevant hashtags.
ChatGPT Responds With: Five complete, ready-to-post Facebook posts — each with the right tone, length (2-3 sentences), a call to action, and 3-5 relevant hashtags like #NEColorado #Pioneer #PlantingSeason #Haxtun. Copy, paste, and post — one per day.
3

Create Graphics for Your Posts

Make eye-catching images to go with your posts.

You Type: Create a simple, clean graphic that says "Spring Planting Season is Here" with a background of a planter in a corn field. Use green and gold colors. Make it square for Facebook/Instagram.
ChatGPT Responds With: A professional square graphic with the text and imagery you described. Download it and attach it to your Facebook post. It'll look like you hired a graphic designer.
4

Plan Short Video Content

Video gets the most engagement on social media. ChatGPT can help you plan what to shoot.

You Type: I want to start making short videos (30-60 seconds) for our farm's Facebook page. I'm not a video expert — just using my phone. Give me 5 easy video ideas I can film around the farm this month. For each one, tell me: what to film, what to say, and how long it should be. Keep it simple.
ChatGPT Responds With: Five specific video ideas like: (1) Walk along a planter during spring prep — 30 seconds showing you checking row units, with a caption about getting ready for the season. (2) A 45-second tour of your seed warehouse showing Pioneer bags stacked and ready. (3) A quick sunrise shot from the cab with a "good morning from the farm" message. Each includes exactly what to film and what to say.
5

Create a Monthly Content Calendar

Get organized so you always know what to post.

You Type: Create a social media content calendar for my farm business for the month of April. I want to post 3 times per week on Facebook. Mix it up between: product highlights (Pioneer seed), farming tips, community/personal posts, and customer engagement. Give me specific post ideas for each day.
ChatGPT Responds With: A complete calendar with 12 specific post ideas, one for each Monday/Wednesday/Friday in April. Each entry includes the date, the topic, and a one-line description of what to post. Print it out and check them off as you go.

AI is your marketing department, graphic designer, and copywriter — all in one. If you want professional content, ask AI. It's smarter and faster than anyone you could hire.
