Getting Started with Claude

Your go-to AI for business planning, writing, and thinking through decisions. Like a smart consultant available 24/7.

Need a marketing plan? A business letter? A budget breakdown? Just ask Claude. AI gives you a better answer, faster, than anyone you could call.

What Is Claude?

Claude is an AI assistant made by Anthropic. You talk to it by typing, just like texting. It's especially good at:

  • Business planning — marketing plans, budgets, proposals
  • Writing — emails, letters, social media posts, reports
  • Analyzing numbers — cost breakdowns, comparisons, projections
  • Thinking through decisions — pros and cons, strategy, options

It's free to start, and you don't need to download anything. Just go to the website and start typing.

How to Get Started

1

Go to Claude

Open your web browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge — any will work) and go to:

claude.ai

You can also use it on your phone — it works on any device.

2

Create a Free Account

Click "Sign Up" and enter your email address. You can also sign in with your Google account if you have one. The free plan gives you plenty of use for everyday tasks.

3

Start a Conversation

You'll see a text box at the bottom of the screen. Just type what you need, like you're texting someone. Hit Enter or click the Send button.

That's it — there's nothing else to set up. No menus to learn, no buttons to find. Just type and go.

Example: Create a Marketing Plan for Your Business

Let's walk through a real example — creating a marketing plan for a custom harvesting business. Follow along step by step.

1

Tell Claude About Your Business

Start by giving Claude some context. Be specific — the more you share, the better the plan will be.

You Type: I run a small custom harvesting and farm services business in Haxtun, Colorado. We do custom combining for wheat, corn, and milo. We also do CRP shredding and some custom planting. Our customers are mostly farmers in Phillips, Sedgwick, and Yuma counties. I want to grow the business and get more customers next year. Can you help me create a simple marketing plan?
Claude Responds With: A complete marketing plan tailored to your area, including: target audience analysis, recommended marketing channels (local papers, co-op bulletin boards, Facebook), seasonal timing for outreach, budget suggestions, and specific action items for each month.
2

Ask Claude to Get More Specific

Now refine it. Ask follow-up questions — the conversation keeps going.

You Type: That's great. Now write me 3 Facebook posts I could use — one for custom harvest season, one for CRP shredding services, and one announcing early booking discounts for spring planting.
Claude Responds With: Three ready-to-post Facebook posts with professional language, relevant hashtags, and a call to action with your phone number. You can copy and paste them straight to your Facebook page.
3

Get a Flyer or Rate Sheet

Ask Claude to draft written materials you can print or email.

You Type: Now write me a one-page rate sheet I can hand out at the co-op. Include custom combining rates for wheat ($28/acre), corn ($38/acre), and milo ($32/acre). Add CRP shredding at $18/acre and custom planting at $22/acre. Include my phone number: (970) 555-1234.
Claude Responds With: A clean, professional rate sheet layout with all your services, rates, and contact info. You can paste it into Word or Google Docs to print.
4

Plan Your Annual Budget

Claude can help you think through the money side too.

You Type: What would be a reasonable marketing budget for a small custom farming business like mine? I don't want to spend much. Give me a plan for under $500 for the whole year.
Claude Responds With: A month-by-month budget breakdown showing where to spend each dollar — like $50 for boosted Facebook posts in March, $100 for a local newspaper ad before harvest, business cards at Vistaprint for $30, and so on.

Don't spend an hour figuring it out. Ask Claude — it takes 30 seconds. AI is smarter than anyone you could call, and it's available right now.
