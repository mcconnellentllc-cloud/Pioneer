75% of Spread Methodology

All custom operation rates are calculated using the 75% of spread methodology:

Rate = Low + 0.75 Ã— (High - Low)

This positions our cost estimates at the 75th percentile of reported rates, providing a conservative (higher) cost estimate that accounts for quality equipment, experienced operators, and complete service.

Example: Disking

Reported Range$10 - $20 per acre
Spread$10
75% of Spread$7.50
Final Rate$10 + $7.50 = $17.50/acre
